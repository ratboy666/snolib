CH - Hex to String

CH(HEX) will convert the sequence of hexadecimal digits into the corresponding character string. CH is the inverse of HEX.

Uses

LOGIC

See Also

HEX

«CH»≡
         DEFINE('CH(HEX)T,C,N')                                :(CH_END)
*
CH       HEX LEN(2) . T =                                     :F(RETURN)
         C = HI(T)
         &ALPHABET LEN(C) LEN(1) . C
         CH = CH C                                                 :(CH)
*
CH_END
@
