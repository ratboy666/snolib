ORDER - Order characters in string

ORDER(S) will put the characters of its argument into alphabetic order. ORDER('DCBA') returns 'ABCD'.

         &ALPHABET BREAK(T) REM . HIGHS
         ORDER (BREAK(HIGHS) | REM) . S1 = S1 T                 :(ORDER)
